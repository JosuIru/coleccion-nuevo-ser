## FAQ

### General

**Q: Is this game free?**
A: Yes! Core gameplay is 100% free. Optional premium pass ($4.99/mo) provides convenience (2x energy regen, extra slots) but no gameplay advantage.

**Q: Can I play offline?**
A: Partially. You can read books and manage beings offline, but collecting fractals and deploying to crises requires internet.

**Q: Does this drain my battery?**
A: We optimized for battery life. Expect ~5% drain per hour with GPS active. You can disable GPS and play in "desk mode."

**Q: What happens to my web account?**
A: Nothing! The mobile game is completely separate and optional. Your web account remains untouched.

---

### Gameplay

**Q: How do I get more beings?**
A:
1. Read books (syncs from web)
2. Use Frankenstein Lab on web app
3. Fuse beings at Level 5+ (mobile)

**Q: Why is my success probability low?**
A: Your being's attributes don't match the crisis requirements well. Try:
- Using a different being
- Training the being's relevant attributes
- Attempting an easier crisis first

**Q: Can I cancel a deployment?**
A: No, once deployed, the being is committed until the mission ends. Plan carefully!

**Q: What happens if I close the app during a mission?**
A: Nothing! Missions continue in the background. You'll get a notification when complete.

---

### Technical

**Q: Why can't I collect this fractal?**
A: Possible reasons:
- You're more than 50m away (check distance)
- Fractal is on cooldown (check timer)

**Q: App won't load my beings:**

## Tips & Tricks

### Efficiency Tips

1. **Plan your route**: Check map before going out, hit multiple fractals
2. **Stack missions**: Deploy multiple beings at once for efficiency
3. **Read daily**: Consistency bonus (7-day streak = 2x consciousness)
4. **Save energy**: Don't deploy to crises you'll likely fail
5. **Fuse strategically**: Wait until you have clear attribute goals

### Advanced Strategies

1. **Attribute Specialization**: Create beings with 90+ in one attribute for specific crises
2. **Balanced Teams**: Also have generalist beings (60+ in multiple attributes)
3. **Location Scouting**: Identify fractal-rich areas in your neighborhood
4. **Community Coordination**: Work with friends on global crises
5. **Resource Management**: Never let energy cap (use it before it wastes)
